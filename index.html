Our results are correct as of the time we carried out our study (Oct 2017-Jan 2018). Newer version of these apps may have addressed the security issues. Several developers reacted to our security advisories and responded with updates to their apps. However, we did not retest newer versions of the apps.

Please see the About page for further details. Our published, peer-reviewed research paper can be found here.
